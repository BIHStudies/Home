Course Questions & Answers

Is this course available on demand?

Yes. All live sessions are recorded and made available in our learning platform, Canvas Instructure. You may watch the lectures on your own schedule and return to them as many times as you wish.

All course materials will be available from January 12, 2026 through December 10, 2026, giving you nearly a full year to study at your own pace.

How long is each class?

Each live Saturday session is 2 hours long. Each class starts at 10 am EST and finishes at 12 noon EST. Most participants also spend about 1–2 hours per week watching recordings, reading, and reflecting.

How do I attend the live sessions?

All live sessions are held on Zoom. Once enrolled, you will receive the Zoom link and an invitation to Canvas, where recordings, slides, and discussions are located.

If you cannot attend live, recordings are posted in Canvas by Monday.

If you did not received your zoom link contact Gandini dd at monica2 at bihstudie.org

Are there exams or tests?

Yes — but only for those who wish to earn a certificate.

The course includes short quizzes and reflections designed to support learning. These are required only for participants who want a BIHS Certificate of Completion. Others may use them optionally to check their understanding and share their reflections.

What is the format of the course?

Each unit includes live lectures, recordings, slides, readings, faculty-moderated discussions, and optional quizzes and reflections.

Is this course suitable for devotees and congregational members?

Yes. The course is designed to be accessible and meaningful for devotees, congregational members, academics, and spiritual seekers alike.
