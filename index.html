Our Mission

The Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies is an international center for research, education, and dialogue at the intersection of science, philosophy, and the Bhāgavata tradition. Through courses, conferences, and publications, BIHS explores themes from consciousness to cosmology, from ethics to ecology, building bridges between rigorous scholarship and enduring wisdom.

Our Vision

We envision a new era of inquiry in which science and philosophy converge to explore consciousness, investigating its role as both the subject and source of reality. Through this integration, research becomes self-transformative — leading not only to discoveries about the world, but to an awakening of wisdom, compassion, and ethical living.

About Us

Founded on a legacy of rigorous inquiry, BIHS brings together leading thinkers to foster dialogue that bridges the gap between modern science and timeless wisdom traditions.
