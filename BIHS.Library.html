About the Library

Mission: To provide access to unique print and electronic resources that facilitate scholarship in bhagavat studies, philosophy, science, cosmology, and consciousness.

Established in 2022, the library was built with the generous support of Hridayananda Goswami and the Tamal Krishna Goswami Legacy Trust. It serves as a vibrant center where the wisdom of the Bhagavat tradition meets contemporary metaphysics. We are proud to house the distinguished Edwin Bryant Collection of over 2,400 rare print resources.

Library Policies & Guidelines

Please help us maintain a quiet and productive study space.

Environment

Help us maintain a quiet and productive study space.

  • Quiet Zone: Please maintain silence.
  • Mobile Devices: Silence phones; take calls outside.
  • Food/Drink: No open containers or food near books.
  • Group Study: Please inquire with staff first.

Special Collections

Due to their value, physical condition, and uniqueness, Special Collections materials must be treated with the utmost care.

  • In-House Only: Materials do not circulate.
  • Care: Do not mark, tear, or highlight pages.
  • Reshelving: Please place used books on the cart.
  • Handling: Ask staff for special item assistance.

Services

  • Scanning: Free (Save to flash drive or email).
  • Computers: Access to BIHS catalogue available.
  • A/V: Rolling TV stand & laptop hookup available.
  • Copyright: Adherence to laws required.
